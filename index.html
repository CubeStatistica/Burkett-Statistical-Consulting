Our Story

Founded in 2011 during Craig's Masters degree, BSC focused on hypothesis testing and statistical inference. BSC expanded, relocated to Ottawa, and became a collaborative team effort. Projects at BSC extended beyond clinical trials and surveys.

Since its inception, BSC has incorporated and experienced substantial growth. Craig's involvement during his Masters degree was pivotal in establishing BSC in 2011. Today, BSC operates as a team-based venture, undertaking diverse projects beyond clinical trials and surveys.
